VACATIONS

ABOUT TOUR MERCH CONTACT

Vacations are an Australian indie rock band from Newcastle, New South Wales formed in 2015. The band was founded by lead singer Campbell Burns in 2015, and joined by Jake Johnson, Nate Delizzotti and Joseph Van Lier. In 2018, they released their debut studio album Changes. In 2020, they released their second album Forever in Bloom. Vacations gained attention and a following for their relaxed yet emotionally resonant songs that often explore themes of youth, relationships, and self-discovery. The band's music style is often described as indie-pop or indie-rock.
